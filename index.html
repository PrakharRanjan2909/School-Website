About Us

The first school of the Surendranath Griup of Educational Institutions was established in 1883 by Rashtraguru Surendranath Banerjee, one of the earliest leaders of India's Independence Movement. Presently, the Trust is set up by Sir Surendranath to manage the affairs of the educational institutions is responsible for setting up and amanging 5 schools & 4 colleges where young people of every social class, community or linguistic group are educated through the medium of english or regional language. The school is affialiated to CBSE, New Delhi.

The school started its academic session in 1988. With every year it made a legendary progress and the school was upgraded to Senior Secondary level in 1997. Presently, SCS is where it aspired to be and it shares its eminence with every student, staff, parents and well wishers.

Recent Activities

World Population Day

July 11, 2021

World Population Day is an annual event celebrated on 11th July to raise awareness of global population issues. The day was celebrated with great enthusiasm by the students of Surendranath Centenary school to mark the importance of the World Population Day. Children from classes 6 to 9 participated in various activities like slogan writing, drawing, reciting poems, speech etc. They also prepared ppt on Data Analysis to highlight population growth and it's bad effects. Short messages were delivered by students and teachers. The main goal of the day was to focus the attention of the world on the importance of Population issues.

No Plastic Day

July 3, 2021

Surendranath Centenary school organised a campaign on 3rd July to create awareness about the hazards of plastics. The campaign was named 'Say No To Plastics '. Classes VI to VIII participated eagerly to make the campaign successful. Banners with slogans were displayed in their localities where they picked up plastic bags and cleaned up the litters. Children also made posters showing the hazards of polythene bags used in the society. Many other activities like speech, dance, songs were performed on the topic. A beautiful poem was composed by Harshvi Shah of std VII. Children also made paper bags and cloth bags for their use.

Van Mahotsav

July 3, 2021

Vanmahotsav, a week long festival of tree plantation is organised every year in the first week of July across India. In an earnest attempt to lend a green touch to the environment and sensitise the students about their role in conserving it, Surendranath Centenary school celebrated Vanmahotsav week . The focus of the Celebration was towards forming a personal connection between the students and trees through a process of experience, reflection and creation. The school authority took an initiative by planting the saplings of Jacaranda trees and many more to maintain the greenery and beauty in the school premises.
